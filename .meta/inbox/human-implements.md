# README: human-as-implementer note

Dropped from meta/ retro (2026-05-04).

The philset lifecycle (assess → draft → ship → review) works even when
the human is the one doing the implementation, not Claude. The LinkedIn
branding session proved this — Phil was updating LinkedIn while Claude
reviewed PDFs against the design doc.

The README's /ship section should note this. Something like: "the workflow
still supports iterative development and review, even when the human is
the one doing the development." The design doc is the spec, /review
compares the artifact against it, regardless of who built the artifact.
